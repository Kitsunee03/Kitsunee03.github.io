## About this project

Shadow Tactics is a top-down stealth strategy game developed in Unity. Players must carefully plan their approach using a team of characters with unique skill sets.

### Key Features
- Multiple playable characters with unique abilities
- Stealth-based gameplay mechanics
- Mission-based level structure
- AI patrol systems
